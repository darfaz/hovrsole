FAQ

How does the suspension system actually work?+
Three integrated systems: (1) A rigid outer frame/exoskeleton that forms the shoe's structural shell. (2) A silicone ankle grip that "catches" and suspends the leg from above. (3) A trampoline-like membrane under the foot that flexes dynamically instead of compressing. Together, they redirect your body weight through the frame to the sole's edges ‚Äî bypassing the bottom of your foot.
